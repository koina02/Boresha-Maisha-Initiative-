About Boresha Maisha

Transforming lives through education and employment

Our Mission

Boresha Maisha Initiative, under Global Student Nest, provides skills training, job placements, and mentorship to uplift vulnerable individuals and communities.

We believe in creating sustainable change by equipping people with marketable skills and connecting them with employment opportunities.
